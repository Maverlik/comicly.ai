<threat_model>
- **T-08-05 Threat:** Automated clients abuse OAuth/auth, profile writes, AI text, or generation endpoints. **Mitigation:** Add per-client in-process rate limits for sensitive route patterns.
- **T-08-06 Threat:** Security headers are missing from production API responses. **Mitigation:** Add middleware for `X-Content-Type-Options`, `Referrer-Policy`, `X-Frame-Options`, `Permissions-Policy`, and production HSTS when secure.
- **T-08-07 Threat:** Rate limiting breaks normal local tests or health checks. **Mitigation:** Scope limits to sensitive endpoints, leave `/health` and `/ready` usable, and make limits configurable for tests.
- **T-08-08 Threat:** Serverless instances bypass global rate counters. **Mitigation:** Document in-process limits as best-effort MVP protection and keep future Redis/platform/WAF hardening deferred.
</threat_model>
