<threat_model>
- **Threat:** Coins are debited but generated page is not persisted. **Mitigation:** Persist job/page failed status and issue idempotent refund on downstream failure.
- **Threat:** Retry after timeout double-charges or double-generates. **Mitigation:** Check existing job by idempotency key before side effects and replay completed outcome.
- **Threat:** User generates into another user's comic/page. **Mitigation:** Resolve comic/page through owner-scoped service queries before job/page mutation.
- **Threat:** External side effects happen inside one untestable route transaction. **Mitigation:** Isolate orchestration in a service with fake provider/storage clients.
</threat_model>
